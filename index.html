I am a first-year PhD student in Machine Learning at the H. Milton Stewart School of Industrial & Systems Engineering (ISyE), Georgia Tech. I'm fortunate to have received the Ronald J. and Carol T. Beerman Fellowship, and the ISyE Beerman President's Fellowship.

Previously, I earned a Bachelor of Statistics (B.Stat) degree in 2022, and a Master of Statistics (M.Stat) degree (with Theoretical Statistics specialization) in 2024, both from the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata.

My research interest lies in the intersection of statistics, machine learning, and optimization. I am interested in theory, methodologies, and their applications that make sense. Prior to joining Georgia Tech, I have mostly worked on high-dimensional classification (with Prof. Subhajit Dutta), clustering (with Prof. Swagatam Das), and conditional independence testing (with Prof. Eugene Katsevich).
